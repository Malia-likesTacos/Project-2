Artist Background

Millman was born in 1961 in Brooklyn, New York. Her mother was a seamstress while her father owned a pharmacy business. She received her Bachelor of Art degree from The State University of New York at Albany. Millman majored in English with a minor in Russian literature.

Debbie as a child

Artist Realization

While in university, Millman was a part of the school's newspaper and eventually became the arts and features editor. While she worked in this role, she realized she had an interest in design.
