Artist Influences

Millman is influenced by visual communication. She believes all of these messages from visual communication are inscribed in humanity. Millman is also influenced by the mystery of the true meaning of design.

Millman's Historic Phone Call

Artist Inspiration

Millman is inspired and strives to create typographic art. An example of typographic art that inspires Millman is Robert Indian and his LOVE Sculpture. She thinks it is one of the most iconic text-based messages of the 20th century.

Indiana's LOVE Sculpture

Artist Style

Millman combines typography with typography. She likes to keep her work bold, and text-based. Her works usually evolve a heavy repetition of words and are filled with lots of color. She gives her more texture. She uses crayons and colored pencils to emphasis both this texture and color.
