Artist Introduction

Debbie Millman was born in 1961. She has published 6 books and is the founder of the 2005 podcast “Design Matters”. It is one of the first and longest running podcasts in the world. She is a frequent speaker on design and branding at TED. Millman is named “one of the most influential designers working today” by Graphic Design USA. She is a former president of AIGA.
