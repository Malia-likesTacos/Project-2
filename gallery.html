Burger King Logo

Burger King needed to revive its brand and give consumers a new reason to come back. Debbie states: “We [Burger King] did worldwide consumer testing and it turned out to be more effective; people liked this version better. Still, I was horribly harassed by this rebranding; I was nicknamed 'She Devil' for this logo!”

New York Hot 97 Logo

The goal was to transform the dance music radio station into the world's first hip-hop radio station. It is one of her earliest works. Millman says she ended up making this logo by accident. She was given this project as a group, however the other 2 people she was with did not like each other and they left. So, she ended up taking over the project. It's still the logo today.

Self Portrait as Your Traitor

Self Portrait as Your Traitor is one of the 6 books written by Millman. Throughout the book, multiple essays and poems are spread out using Millman's style.Various pages show Millman's use of handwritten typography and vibrant colors. This book represents how important expressiveness is to Millman.
